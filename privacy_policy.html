Privacy Policy

Porcelain Pages Journal

Effective date: June 17, 2026

App package name: com.porcelainpages.journalkit

This Privacy Policy describes how Porcelain Pages Journal ("the App," "we," or "us") handles information when you use our Android application.

Summary: The App is an offline journaling tool. Journal content and preferences are stored on your device. We do not operate user accounts, and we do not collect or receive your journal content on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information for transmission to our servers.

The App does not require account creation, login, or registration.

The App does not include third-party advertising SDKs in this version.

The App does not include third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs in this version.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 through our own services, because we do not collect user data on our servers.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

To provide journaling features, the App stores data locally on your device, including:

  • Journal entry titles and content
  • Template field values you enter
  • Tags, favorite status, and archive status
  • Theme preference (light, dark, or system)
  • Onboarding completion status

This information is stored in a local database and local app preferences on your device. It remains on your device unless you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the App.

3. How We Use Information

Locally stored information is used only to operate App features, such as displaying, editing, searching, favoriting, archiving, and deleting your journal entries, and applying your appearance settings.

We do not use your journal content for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your journal content with third parties.

We do not transmit your journal content to our servers in this version of the App.

The App does not provide cloud sync features in this version.

5. Network Access

Core journaling features work offline. The App is designed for local use and does not require internet access for creating, editing, searching, or managing entries.

Based on the current App configuration, the App does not declare the Android INTERNET permission and does not perform routine network communication for core features.

6. Android Permissions

The App is designed to work without dangerous permissions. In the current release, the App does not request permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Microphone / audio recording
  • Location (fine or coarse)
  • Contacts
  • Read or write external storage
  • Manage external storage
  • Read media (images, video, or audio)
  • Post notifications
  • Query all packages

The App may include standard AndroidX components that use internal, app-specific permissions required by the Android system. These are not used to access your personal journal content for transmission to us.

7. Android Backup and Device Transfer

The App enables Android backup settings (allowBackup is enabled). Depending on your device settings and Google account configuration, your local journal database and app preferences may be included in:

  • Android cloud backup (if enabled on your device)
  • Device-to-device transfer (if you use Android device transfer features)

These processes are managed by Android and your device settings, not by a cloud sync service operated by us. Backup behavior may vary by device manufacturer and Android version.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

You can manage your data inside the App:

  • Delete individual journal entries
  • Clear all local journal data from Settings > Data Management

Uninstalling the App removes local App data from your device. Data previously included in Android backup may remain according to your device and Google account backup settings until you delete those backups.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children on our servers.

If you believe a child has contacted us by email, please contact us and we will respond appropriately.

10. Security

Your journal data is stored locally on your device. Device-level protections (such as your screen lock and device encryption settings) help protect data on your device.

No method of electronic storage is completely secure. We encourage you to use device security features and keep your device software up to date.

11. International Users

Because we do not collect or store your journal content on our servers in this version, cross-border data transfers by us do not apply to your journal content.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if App features, permissions, or data practices change. When we update the policy, we will revise the effective date above. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

If we introduce features that collect or share data (for example, analytics, advertising, cloud sync, or export/import to remote services), we will update this policy before or when those features are released.
